#reddit-anime - Rules and Information

Rules

  1. Don't Spoil things for people. If in doubt, ask first and move it to a query.
  2. No public away messages. Learn it, love it.
  3. No excessive usage of /nick (excessive is up to channel ops to define)
  4. Don't append things to your nick to indicate where you are or what computer you're using. No one cares and it's annoying.
  5. No threats or personal attacks (the ops have a sense of humor, don't worry)
  6. When an op tells you do drop a subject, drop it. It's all about keeping harmony.
  7. English is the preferred language of the channel, but of course there will be incidental Japanese.
  8. Don't spam the channel. (Tons of short lines, copypasta, etc.) randum.txt here withstanding - airen
  9. Don't roleplay. Roleplaying is stupid and smacks of Gaia, and Gaia is horrible.
  10. Don't ask for ops. Asking for ops is putting on a sign that says "Ban me".
  11. Don't use auto re-join. A kick is a timeout (take a moment to reflect on why you were kicked.) Auto-rejoin breaks that. That's asking for a kick to be turned into a ban.
  12. If your link isn't safe for work, or is questionable, prepend/append NSFW to your link. Some of us have jobs we'd like to keep.
  13. If your link is gore, or is questionable, prepend/append NSFL to your link. Some of us have lunches we'd like to keep.
  14. If your link contains spoilers, tell people that a) there are spoilers and b) what they might be spoiling (e.g. http://whatever.com/img.png thing spoilers)
  15. Ban evasion will lead to another ban. If you keep trying to join despite being banned, your behavior will be brought up with Freenode staff.
  16. If you complain about how much you dislike the channel and how much you don't want to be here, you will be given help leaving.
  17. We are not your personal recommendation service. If all you do is join and ask for recommendations, you're going to have a bad time.
  18. Don't append things to your nick to indicate team affiliation, holiday celebration, or the like.
  19. Your droids aren't welcome here. We have one bot and we don't want any more of them.
If you post spoilers, you'll be banned. From the channel and from /r/anime. Simple as that. No second chances.
Fake spoilers are still spoilers. The ops can't watch every show and know what's contextual or not. Don't fake spoil things either.
Spoilers for things that aren't anime are still spoilers.
There is no expiration period for spoilers. Old spoilers are still spoilers
Don't hide your spoilers with colors. Not all IRC clients will support that. Just don't spoil things.
Have thick skin. We joke around a lot. That doesn't mean abuse is okay though, if you feel abused, report it to a channel op.
This list of rules is not exhaustive and if the channel ops deem any behavior unacceptable they can and will take action.
