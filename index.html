##weeabros - Rules and Information

Rules

  1. Don't Spoil things for people. If in doubt, ask first and move it to a query.
  2. No public away messages. Learn it, love it.
  3. No excessive usage of /nick (excessive is up to channel ops to define)
  4. Don't append things to your nick to indicate where you are, what computer you're using, team affiliation, holiday celebration, etc.
  5. No threats, personal attacks, or encouraging suicide (the ops have a sense of humor, don't worry)
  6. When an op tells you to drop a subject, drop it. It's all about keeping harmony.
  7. Don't spam the channel. (Tons of short lines, copypasta, etc.)
  8. Don't roleplay.
  9. If your link isn't safe for work, or is questionable, prepend/append NSFW to your link. Some of us have jobs we'd like to keep.
  10. If your link is gore, or is questionable, prepend/append NSFL to your link. Some of us have lunches we'd like to keep.
  11. If your link contains spoilers, tell people that a) there are spoilers and b) what they might be spoiling (e.g. http://whatever.com/img.png thing spoilers)
  12. Ban evasion will lead to another, longer ban. If you keep trying to join despite being banned, your behavior will be brought up with Freenode staff.
  13. If you complain about how much you dislike the channel and how much you don't want to be here, you will be given help leaving.
  14. Your droids aren't welcome here. We have one bot and we don't want any more of them.

Spoilers are verboten

If you post spoilers, you'll be banned. Simple as that. No second chances.
Fake spoilers are still spoilers. The ops can't watch every show and know what's contextual or not. Don't fake spoil things either.
There is no expiration period for spoilers. Old spoilers are still spoilers
Don't hide your spoilers with colors. Not all IRC clients will support that. Just don't spoil things.

Memes

Don't import your memes here en masse. The point of this channel is communication, not spamming people with the latest internet trend.

Invitations

This is a not a private channel, but it also isn't advertised. Feel free to invite people, but if they make trouble, you'll be held accountable for their actions.
Use your judgment.

Last but not least

Have thick skin. We joke around a lot. That doesn't mean abuse is okay though, if you feel abused, report it to a channel op.
This list of rules is not exhaustive and if the channel ops deem any behavior unacceptable they can and will take action.
